How to Play Chess Conquest

Welcome to Chess Conquest, a strategic game where you compete to either capture the opponent's king or conquer the central hill!

  • Objective: Win by capturing the opponent's king or holding the central hill (yellow square) for 45 seconds.
  • Moving Pieces: Click a piece to select it (green highlight), then click a highlighted square to move. You can deselect by clicking the piece again.
  • Piece Movement:
    • Pawn: Moves 1 square in any direction (up, down, left, right) or diagonally to capture.
    • Knight: Moves in an L-shape (2 squares in one direction, 1 square perpendicular).
    • Bishop: Moves diagonally up to 5 squares.
    • Rook: Moves horizontally or vertically up to 5 squares.
    • Queen: Moves in any direction (horizontal, vertical, diagonal) up to 5 squares.
    • King: Moves 1 square in any direction.
  • Cooldowns: After moving, pieces have a cooldown: 5 seconds on grass (light green), 10 seconds on forest (dark green). You cannot move a piece until its cooldown ends (red overlay fades).
  • Hill Conquest: Move a piece onto the central hill (yellow square) and hold it for 45 seconds to win. The remaining time is shown in the progress bar above the status.
  • Inactivity Timeout: You must move a piece within 90 seconds, or you lose the game. If both players are inactive, the game ends with no winner. Timers for each team are displayed (e.g., "Team 0: 90s | Team 1: 90s").
  • Queue System: Only one game can be active at a time. If a game is ongoing, you’ll be placed in a queue. When the current game ends, the next two players in the queue start immediately. The queue size is displayed (e.g., "Players in queue: 0").
  • Spectator Mode: If you’re in the queue, you can spectate the current game but cannot interact.
  • Shrines: Gray squares are shrines. Moving onto a shrine has a 20% chance to delete the piece and an 80% chance to duplicate it to the right (if space is available).
  • Winning: Win by capturing the opponent’s king, holding the hill for 45 seconds, or having the most pieces after 10 minutes. The winner receives a random SOL prize from our Solana faucet (0.001 SOL: 60%, 0.005 SOL: 30%, 0.01 SOL: 10%, rarest), but only if at least 10 moves are made in the match. Enjoy earning SOL!
  • Game Over: After a game ends, you have 10 seconds to choose "Exit" or "Go to Queue" before the prompt disappears.
